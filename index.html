About Organization

AIESEC in India is a non-political, independent, not-for-profit organization in consultative status with the United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC), affiliated with the UN DGC (formerly DPI), and recognized by UNESCO. It serves as a global platform for young people to explore and develop their leadership potential, operating in partnership with over 100 countries to facilitate international professional and volunteering experiences.
